Welcome to Siri

Try the following commands:

  1. hello: Siri will greet you
  2. some temperature: Siri will tell you what to wear
  3. count: Siri will tell you how many questions you've asked so far
  4. game: Siri will play a game with you
  5. what is my record for, then say a difficulty: Siri will tell you your record for the difficulty
  6. try to guess the secret number. Siri will tell you if you get it
  7. what movie should I watch: Siri will recommend a movie
  8. joke: Siri will tell you a joke
  9. sum followed by a number: Siri will tell you the sum of every number between 0 and that number, including that number. (WARNING. NUMBERS OVER 27,500 MAY TAKE AN EXTENDED PERIOD OF TIME TO CALCULATE. NUMBERS OVER 1,000,000 ARE NOT ACCEPTED).
  10. factor followed by a number: Siri will tell you the product of every number between 0 and that number, including that number.(WARNING. NUMBERS OVER 175 ARE NOT ACCEPTED).
  11. celsius followed by a number: Siri will convert it to fahrenheit.
  12. fahrenheit followed by a number: Siri will convert it to celsius.
  13. reverse followed by a string: Siri will tell you the string reversed
  14. what was my last entry: Siri will tell you what your last entry was.
